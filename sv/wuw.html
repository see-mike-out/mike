Women's Annual Unpaid Work Hour

The graph below shows women's unpaid work hours of OECD countries. If you point a bar by hovering, clicking, or touching, you can check the specific value. The values are calculated as average annual work hour multplied by gender wage gap. You can sort the graph by clicking legends which are located at the corners of the graph.

Source: OECD Stats
