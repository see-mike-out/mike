Seoul's Four Seasons

South Korea is known ta have four seasons (spring, summer, fall, and winter), and many Korean students learn so. However, many people feel the climate in a different way: two-seasons with periodic disasters. Thus, I re-defined seasons in a way people actually feel: Fine Dust (미세먼지), Extremely Hot (폭염), Season-Change (환절기), and Extremely Cold (혹한).

Langauge: Korean (Translatable)
